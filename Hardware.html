Whats inside a computer?

Here are all the parts.


Picture Name Description
Hard Drive The hard drive stores all of the data for the computer. It is very solid, and costs about 50$ to 150$
Power Supply The power supply is what powers the computer. It is very solid, and cost about 50$ to 100$
DVD Drive Plays A DVD and Stores A DVD. It is pretty Solid, and costs about 40$ to 60$
Mother Board The brain of the computer. It is pretty Solid, and cost about 100$ to 300$
RAM Stores Random storage to be sent to the hard drive later on. It is somewhat solid, and It costs about 20$ to about 50$
